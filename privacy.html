Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 · Effective immediately · Version 2.0

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Datta AI collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our services at datta-ai.com or through our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Name, email address, and encrypted password when you register
  • Chat data: Messages and conversations stored to provide chat history
  • Usage data: Message count, features used, models selected, timestamps
  • Device information: Device type, OS, browser, and IP address for security
  • Payment information: Plan name, billing cycle, transaction IDs only. Card and UPI details are processed by Razorpay and never stored on our servers
  • Voice data: Processed locally on your device via Web Speech API. We do not record or store your voice

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Datta AI service
  • To manage your account, subscription, and usage limits
  • To send transactional emails (welcome on signup, confirmation after payment)
  • To provide customer support
  • To detect and prevent abuse and unauthorized access
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your conversations for advertising.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Data stored on MongoDB Atlas with encryption at rest
  • All communication uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Passwords are hashed with bcrypt — we cannot view your password
  • Secure JWT authentication with token expiry
  • Payments handled by Razorpay (PCI-DSS compliant)
  • Rate limiting and abuse prevention protect all accounts

4. Subscription and Payment

  • Payment processed by Razorpay — India's leading payment gateway
  • Payment signatures verified server-side before activating any subscription
  • We store plan name, billing cycle, and payment ID only — not card or bank details
  • Confirmation email sent to your registered address after successful payment
  • Manage subscription from Settings in the app

5. Email Communication

We only send emails when necessary:

  • Welcome email: Once when you create your account
  • Subscription confirmation: After each successful payment
  • Email verification: When you register
  • Password reset: Only when you request it

We do not send marketing emails or promotional spam.

6. Data Retention

  • Account and chat data retained while your account is active
  • Delete your account anytime from Settings — data removed within 30 days
  • Payment records retained up to 7 years for legal compliance

7. Third-Party Services

  • Groq AI: Processes chat messages to generate AI responses
  • Razorpay: All payment processing
  • MongoDB Atlas: Cloud database storage
  • Zoho Mail: Transactional email delivery
  • Tavily: Powers live web search in responses
  • Google OAuth: Optional sign-in with Google

8. Children's Privacy

Datta AI is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us at admin@datta-ai.com immediately.

9. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update information from Settings
  • Deletion: Delete account and all data from Settings
  • Portability: Request your chat history
  • Objection: Contact us to object to specific data processing

10. Cookies and Local Storage

Datta AI uses browser local storage to save preferences (theme, language, model). No tracking cookies, no advertising pixels, no third-party trackers.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via email. The "Last updated" date above will always reflect the latest version.
