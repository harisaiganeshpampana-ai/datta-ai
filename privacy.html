Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 April 2026

Quick summary: We store only what's needed to run Datta AI (your account, apps you build, usage). We don't sell your data. Your published apps are public when you publish them. Everything else stays private.

Who We Are

Datta AI is operated by LR AI Technologies, based in India. Contact: hello@datta-ai.com

What We Collect

Account Information

When you sign up, we collect:

  • Your email address or Google account info
  • Username you choose
  • Password (encrypted, we cannot see it)

App Data

When you use Datta AI Builder:

  • Your prompts — what you type to build apps
  • Generated code — the apps AI creates for you
  • Published apps — code you explicitly publish becomes public at your unique URL
  • Usage counts — how many apps you build per day (for plan limits)

Technical Data

  • IP address (for security, rate limiting)
  • Browser type, device info
  • Basic analytics (page views, features used)

Payment Data

Payments are processed by Razorpay. We store only: subscription plan, start date, renewal date. We never see your card details.

How We Use Your Data

  • To generate apps via AI (prompts go to Gemini API and similar providers)
  • To save your apps so you can come back to them
  • To serve your published apps to the public at their URLs
  • To enforce plan limits (builds per day)
  • To contact you about your account or important updates
  • To improve Datta AI (which features work, which don't)

What We Don't Do

  • We don't sell your data to anyone
  • We don't share your prompts or code with advertisers
  • We don't read your private apps for business purposes
  • We don't train AI on your private code without permission

Third-Party Services

To provide our service, we use:

  • Google Gemini — AI that generates your code (prompts are sent but not stored by Google for training)
  • MongoDB Atlas — secure database for your account and apps
  • Razorpay — payment processing
  • Google OAuth — if you log in with Google
  • Render / Vercel — hosting infrastructure

Published Apps

When you click Publish, your app's code becomes publicly viewable at its URL (e.g., datta-ai.com/apps/yourname/my-app). Anyone with the link can see and use your app. Don't publish apps containing secrets like API keys, passwords, or private data.

You can unpublish or delete any app at any time from your dashboard.

Data Retention

  • Account: Kept as long as your account is active
  • Apps: Kept until you delete them
  • Chat history: Kept for 90 days, then auto-deleted
  • Deleted accounts: All data permanently removed within 30 days

Your Rights

You can at any time:

  • View all your data (in Settings → Export Data)
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your apps and code
  • Unpublish any public app
  • Cancel your subscription

Children's Privacy

Datta AI is intended for users aged 13 and above. If you're under 18, please use Datta AI with parental guidance. We don't knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Security

We take security seriously:

  • All traffic encrypted with HTTPS
  • Passwords hashed using bcrypt (we can't see them)
  • Database access restricted and logged
  • Regular security audits

No system is 100% secure. If you discover a vulnerability, please report it to us at security@datta-ai.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as Datta AI grows. Major changes will be emailed to registered users. Continued use after changes means you accept them.

Contact Us
