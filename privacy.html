2. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide and improve the Datta AI service

  • Analyze aggregate usage patterns to improve our service

We do NOT use your personal chat data to train AI models. We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Where Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored on MongoDB Atlas servers (United States). We use encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and encryption at rest for sensitive data.

Security Measures

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — we never store plain text passwords
  • Authentication uses JWT tokens with secure expiry
  • All communications use HTTPS encryption
  • Database access is restricted and monitored
  • We conduct regular security reviews

Data Retention

  • Chat history: Retained while your account is active
  • Account data: Deleted within 30 days of account deletion request
  • Payment records: Retained for 7 years as required by Indian tax law
  • Server logs: Retained for 90 days for security purposes

5. Children's Privacy

Datta AI is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately. Parents or guardians who believe their child under 13 has provided us with personal data should contact us at admin@datta-ai.com.

6. Your Rights

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Portability: Request your chat history in a machine-readable format
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw consent to data processing (this will require account deletion)
  • Grievance Redressal: Lodge a complaint with us or the Data Protection Board of India

To exercise these rights, email admin@datta-ai.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser local storage (not traditional cookies) to store:

  • Your authentication token (to keep you logged in)
  • Theme preference (dark/light/eye care)
  • Font size and display settings
  • Selected AI model preference

This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers except the authentication token for API requests.

8. Voice and Image Data

Voice

Voice input is processed entirely by your device's Web Speech API (provided by your browser or operating system). We do not record, transmit, or store your voice audio. Only the text transcription of your speech is sent to our servers.

Images

When you upload an image for analysis, it is temporarily transmitted to Google's Gemini API for processing. Images are not permanently stored on our servers. The image reference is saved as a text label (e.g., "📷 filename.jpg") in your chat history.

9. Data Transfers

As Datta AI uses servers and third-party services located in the United States, your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using our service, you consent to this transfer. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place through our agreements with service providers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification at least 15 days before they take effect. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
