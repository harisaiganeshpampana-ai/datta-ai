Privacy Policy

We care about your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user of Datta AI.

Last updated: March 18, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Datta AI, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Your username and password (stored securely as a hashed value).
  • Chat data: Messages you send to Datta AI and responses generated, stored to provide chat history.
  • Usage data: Information about how you use the service, including features accessed and frequency of use.
  • Device data: Browser type, operating system, and IP address for security and analytics purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Datta AI service.
  • Authenticate your identity and keep your account secure.
  • Store and display your chat history across sessions.
  • Analyze usage trends to improve features and performance.
  • Respond to your support requests or feedback.

We do not sell your personal data to any third parties.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers hosted via Render. Passwords are never stored in plain text — they are hashed using industry-standard encryption.

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Chat History

Your conversations with Datta AI are saved to your account to provide continuity across sessions. You can delete individual chats or all chat history at any time from Settings → Account → Delete All Chats.

Deleted chats are permanently removed from our servers and cannot be recovered.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

Datta AI uses browser localStorage to store your authentication token, preferences (theme, language, font size), and session data. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to third parties.

We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

6. Third-Party Services

Datta AI uses the following third-party services that may process data:

  • Anthropic API / AI provider: Your messages are sent to an AI provider to generate responses. Please review their privacy policy for how they handle data.
  • Pollinations AI: Used for image generation. Image prompts are sent to their servers.
  • Google Fonts: Fonts are loaded from Google's CDN.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time.
  • Update your username and password from Settings.
  • Export your chat history (feature coming soon).

To delete your account, go to Settings → Account → Delete Account, or contact us directly.

8. Children's Privacy

Datta AI is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Datta AI after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact the Datta AI team through the app or reach out via your registered account.
