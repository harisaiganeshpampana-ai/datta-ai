Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Datta AI collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Datta AI, we may collect the following information:

  • Account information: Username, email address, and password (encrypted)
  • Chat data: Messages you send and receive within the app
  • Usage data: How often you use the app, features you use
  • Device information: Device type, operating system, browser type
  • Payment information: Processed securely through Razorpay/Stripe — we do not store card details

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Datta AI service
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions
  • To send important service notifications and updates
  • To personalize your experience using AI memory features
  • To analyze usage patterns and fix bugs
  • To prevent fraud and ensure security

3. AI and Chat Data

Your conversations with Datta AI are processed by Groq AI infrastructure. Chat history is stored securely in our database and is only accessible to you. We may use anonymized, aggregated data to improve our service.

The AI memory feature stores facts about you to personalize responses. You can view and delete this data anytime in Settings → Memory.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on MongoDB Atlas with industry-standard encryption. We use HTTPS for all data transmission. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and are never stored in plain text.

5. Third Party Services & Attribution

Datta AI uses the following third-party services:

  • Meta Llama Models — AI language models (Llama 3.1, 3.3, Llama 4) used under Meta's Llama Community License
  • Groq Inc. — AI inference infrastructure for running language models
  • MongoDB Atlas — Secure database storage
  • Razorpay / Stripe — Payment processing
  • Pollinations AI — Image generation
  • Tavily — Web search functionality
  • Google OAuth — Optional Google sign-in
  • Gemma (Google) — AI language model used under Google's Gemma Terms of Use

Datta AI is an independent product and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Meta, Google, or Groq. Each service has their own privacy policy and terms of use.

AI Model Attribution: The AI responses in Datta AI are powered by open-source language models including Meta's Llama series and Google's Gemma, accessed through Groq's API infrastructure.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in these cases:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect the rights and safety of our users
  • With service providers who help us operate Datta AI (under strict confidentiality)

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data at any time
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your chat history
  • Opt out of AI memory features
  • Request correction of inaccurate data

To exercise these rights, go to Settings in the app or contact us at the email below.

8. Cookies

Datta AI uses local storage (not cookies) to save your preferences like theme, language, and login session. No tracking cookies are used.

9. Children's Privacy

Datta AI is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting a notice in the app. Continued use of Datta AI after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
