Common questions

How do credits actually work?
Every message you send to AI uses credits based on the model. Llama 3.3 (Fast) costs 1 credit. Gemini 2.5 Pro costs 5. GPT-4o costs 8. Pick the model that fits your task — use Fast for quick tweaks, Pro for production apps. Credits reset every 24 hours.
Why multiple AI models?
Different models excel at different things. Llama is fast & cheap for simple changes. Gemini Flash is great for general work. Claude Sonnet writes beautiful UI. Gemini Pro builds complete production apps. GPT-4o handles the hardest problems. You get them all in one place.
Do unused credits roll over?
No, credits reset every 24 hours so you get a fresh allowance daily. This keeps pricing predictable and lets you build consistently every day.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from Settings → Subscription. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period, then auto-downgrade to Free plan. Built apps stay yours forever.
What payment methods?
UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), all debit/credit cards, and wallets — all through Razorpay. Secure & instant.
Do I own the apps I build?
100% yes. The code is yours forever. Download it, modify it, sell it, deploy anywhere. Datta AI makes no claim on what you build.
Is there a student discount?
Our Starter plan at ₹49/month is already priced for students. If you can't afford it, email hello@datta-ai.com with your student ID for special pricing.
7-day money-back guarantee?
Yes. If unhappy in the first 7 days, email us for a full refund. After that, you can cancel anytime but no refunds for partial months.
