Frequently asked questions

What counts as an "app build"?
Every time you describe a new app or add major new features, that counts as one build. Minor edits don't count. Updating your published app doesn't count either.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from Settings → Subscription. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period, then downgrade to Free plan. No questions asked.
What payment methods do you accept?
UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), all debit/credit cards, and wallets — all through Razorpay. Indian payments only for now.
Do I own the apps I build?
Yes, 100%. The code is yours. Download it, modify it, sell it — do whatever you want. Datta AI makes no claim on apps you build.
What happens to my apps if I downgrade?
All your built apps stay. You can still download them. On Free plan you won't be able to publish new ones, but existing published apps keep working.
Is there a student discount?
Our Starter plan at ₹29/month is already priced for students. If you need more and can't afford it, email hello@datta-ai.com with your student ID.
Can I get a refund?
If you're unhappy in the first 7 days, email us for a full refund. After that, we don't do refunds but you can cancel anytime.
